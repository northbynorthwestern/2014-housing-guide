Bobb/McCulloch Hall

2305 Sheridan Road

Two distinct buildings connect to form Bobb-McCulloch Hall. All floors are connected, excluding the ground floor of both halls which typically houses a single gender. Freshmen and sophomores of various majors and personality types live in Bobb. Each floor has its own small lounge while the ground floors of both Bobb and McCulloch have larger lounges. Bobb-McCulloch is centrally located near Tech, the fraternity quad and two of Northwesternâ€™s three on-campus gyms.
